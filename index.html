Phantom Wallet | A Friendly Crypto Solana


Phantom is Solana friendly crypto wallet that let you store, buy, send, receive, swap tokens, and collect NFTs in a secured way. This wallet lets you trade and earn SOL easily and quickly in case you have created your profile on it. Anyone can use Phantom Wallet on their mobile and computer on both types of devices by setting up a profile. Phantom can be used on both types of OS phones such as Android and iOS. Else, if you want to use this wallet on a computer, you need to install the Phantom browser extension on it to proceed. Let’s move on to the processes to learn about the sign-in and wallet download process. 

                      

WAY TO PHANTOM WALLET SIGN IN

 

Signing in to a Phantom account is easy. But before that, you need to make sure that you have a Phantom account password or seed phrase details. If you are not sure about how to sign in, do the following:

1.      Open Phantom Wallet mobile application

2.      Else, launch the Phantom browser extension

3.      In the password field, type the password details

4.      Or you can also use the private key details to recover the account

5.      Get to the ‘Sign in’ button and click it to access the wallet

6.      Finally, you have accessed your Phantom Wallet sign in account

 

HOW TO GET PHANTOM APP SOLANA WALLET?

 

Phantom can be used as Solana Wallet. To do that, you can use the Phantom app on your Android or iOS device. Once you install the Phantom app on your mobile, you will be able to proceed with the wallet setup and sign-in process. To install the Phantom app on your mobile, you need to visit the App or Play Store based on the type of mobile that you are using.   

 

HOW TO DOWNLOAD PHANTOM WALLET ON IPHONE?

 

iPhone users who are looking to download the Phantom app on it need to go through the steps that are given below:

1.      On your iPhone, launch the App Store

2.      Now, head to the ‘Search Box’ and tap on it

3.      Type ‘Phantom’ in it and tap on the search icon

4.      Now, find and tap on the ‘Get’ button next to the Phantom app icon

5.      After that, the app will be downloaded on your iPhone

6.      Finally, you can start using the Phantom account on your device  

 

WAY TO PHANTOM NFT WALLET

 

Phantom is a beautiful home for all of your NFTs. You can manage your NFTs with the help of the Phantom Wallet account. You do not need to create a separate account to manage NFTs. You can use the Phantom account to buy and sell NFTs easily and securely.

 

WAY TO PHANTOM WALLET SOLANA DOWNLOAD

 

To download Phantom Wallet, follow these steps:

1.      Open a browser or launch the App or Play Store

2.      Now, discover the Phantom app or extension

3.      Click on the ‘Download’ to start the process

4.      Now, the Phantom Wallet will be downloaded to your device 

 

PHANTOM WALLET EXTENSION EDGE

 

If you are looking to install the Phantom extension on the Edge browser, you need to follow the steps that are given below:

1.      Open Edge on your Windows computer

2.      Now, you need to visit the Phantom.com webpage

3.      From here, go to the extension page and find the extension file

4.      Now, click on the ‘Edge’ icon to start the downloading process
